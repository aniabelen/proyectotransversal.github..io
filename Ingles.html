Solar Energy

Solar energy is obtained from the sun's radiation. It is renewable and abundant. Solar panels are used to convert sunlight into electricity.

Wind Energy

Wind energy is generated by harnessing the power of wind using wind turbines. It is clean and renewable, making it an environmentally friendly option.

Hydroelectric Energy

Hydroelectric energy is produced by harnessing the energy of flowing water. It is reliable and widely used around the world to generate electricity.

Geothermal Energy

Geothermal energy is produced by harnessing the energy of geothermal sources. It is clean and reliable, making it an environmentally friendly option.

Biomass Energy

Biomass energy is produced by harnessing the energy of biomass (Organic matter of living beings). It is clean, economically and renewable, making it an environmentally friendly option.
